Hey there! So, my journey's been a wild ride so far. It all started with crunching numbers as an auditor for a Big 4 firm, then I switched lanes into industrial engineering. The real game-changer came when I set up a couple of tech startups, and that's when I caught the coding bug.

Now, with some help from #100daysofcode and a nifty tool called GPT technology, I'm waist-deep in code. I'm tackling HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and SQL - and loving every minute of it!

Oh, and for a bit of a twist, I've picked up flying a single-engine plane too. Didn't quite manage to get the license, but it's a long story - check it out here.

So, I am basically a tech-loving generalist, always on the lookout for the next big thing. I'm filled with optimism for my millennial peers who are all about true democratization, all backed by perseverance, speed, and unbiased tools like LLMs and Bitcoin. I truly believe this decade will be the most productive in human history. Let's see where this crazy ride takes us!
