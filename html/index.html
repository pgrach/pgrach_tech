ABOUT

Hey there! My career journey has been a wild ride. I started in finance, crunching numbers as an auditor. I then switched into industrial engineering and project management, leading me to launch a couple of startups in the wild west of crypto mining. That's when I caught the builders bug! Trying to build or improve some specific thing that people like is a strong drug.

I recently completed a 12-week Software Developer Bootcamp with JustIT, where I learned critical skills in full-stack development. With some help from #100daysofcode and nifty generative AI tools, I'm now waist-deep in code - tackling HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and SQL. I also picked up flying single-engine planes, though I didn't quite get my license yet.

Originally from Uzbekistan, I've lived and worked across Russia, Luxembourg, and the UK. I'm a trilingual, tech-loving generalist always seeking the next big thing. I'm filled with optimism for my millennial peers who are democratizing technology and information. I believe this decade will be the most productive in human history thanks to unbiased tools like AI and blockchain. Let's see where this crazy ride takes us!
