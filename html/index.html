My way to coding was slow and thorny. Rabithole after rabithole and the 100daysofcode with Replit and JustIT programme provided me with the right tools and structures, and most importantly, the inspiration of a compelling force of programming.

I've had the privilege of working with different corporate industries, from accounting to PET packaging engineering, and founding and leading startups in crypto mining and Earth Observations. As they say - Once a founder, always a founder.

My main focus these days is to get more advanced in coding and web development, focusing on Python and anything around LLM technologies. I truly believe this decade will be the most productive in human history. I'm also bullish about my generation of millennials who truly believe in democratizing (not just announcing democracies here-and-there) with perseverance and speed, powered with non-judgmental and nuanced "no-agenda" LLM tools. That is going to make this decade exciting.
